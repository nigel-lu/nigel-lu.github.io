BC Hydro - Electric Vehicle Charging Station

Constructing better brand identity for BC Hydro by bringing pleasant charging experiences to the local EV owners.

EV charger's different views

Here are a front view and a perspective view of the EV charger. The charger is symmetrical. It features a black matte finished front plate and a metalic ring wrapping around the entire body.

Different color choices of the charger

There are three final color decisions provided for BC Hydro on the charger: white, black, and orginal metalic finish.

LED light ring

The design features a ring of LED light around each of the charger's nozzles. This feature helps with the communication with the users without requiring using words.

Cable Rack

Here is a close-up shot on the cable rack design. The cable rack is intentionally designed in this specific form for easier hanging for the users.

Sketches

Here is a quick view on the sketches of the charger. A lot of iterations were done and presented to the stakeholders, and through which the design team was able to decide on the best suitable design to move forward.
