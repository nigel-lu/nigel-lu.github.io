BC Hydro- Customer Center Presentation Room

A multifunctional audio visual integration system.

Three sample pages over view

Here are an overview of the three pages of samples of the design. We only created these pages as they are the most representative pages of the entire system.

Landing page

The landing page of the system features a large background image and the system's title. The main five functions are listed on the menu bar at the bottom of the page.

Presentation

The presentation page contains multiple smaller screens for displaying the content played from different ends. The users can also adjust the audio volume.

Video conference page

The video conference page allows the users to have full control over their monitoring cameras while being able to check out the content from different views.

Older version evaluation

Before we proceeded to the designing stage, we conducted a heuristic evaluation to understand the problems with the existing system. The evaluation specifically targets the icon and layout design of the system.
