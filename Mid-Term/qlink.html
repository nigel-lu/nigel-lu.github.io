Qlink - Social Q&A App Reimagined

A social Q&A app that matches a user's questions with his/her friends or indirect friends who have the expertise or experience.

Simple sign up process: fill in expertise & specialties

Qlink requires users to fill in keywords of their personal background, specialties and interests. These information are used for distributing only the relevant questions to the users.

Ask question like sending a chat message

Questions asked in Qlink will be sent to the user's friends or friends of a friend. Users type in their questions like sending out a chat message.

Add keywords to questions to get answers from capable hands

After finishing editing the question, the users can easily add keywords by either clicking on the recommended keywords directly or typing in their owns. Questions are distributed to the friends or friends of a friend according to the keywords tagged on.

Receive notification about answer request like receiving text message

Once a user receive an answer request, he/she will also receive a notification message. By clicking on the notification, the user will be directed into the question page to provide answers. The user can also check the question asker's personal information.

Make new connections & keep the old ones

After the user answers a question, he/she will be rewarded with the opportunity to connect with the question asker. If the user viewed the question, his/her profile picture will show up on the question as "Viewed" just like Messenger.
